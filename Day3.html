Effects of Cyberbullying

Ordered List:

  1. Emotional distress
  2. Decline in academic performance
  3. Social withdrawal
  4. Increased risk of mental health issues
  5. Physical health problems

Unordered List:

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Low self-esteem
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Feelings of isolation
